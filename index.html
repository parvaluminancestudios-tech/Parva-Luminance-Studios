About

A studio built
around little light.

Mission

Our mission with ParvaLuma is to create a company that can grow into a community and help spread the little light we have to others by creating work that inspires people.

Story

We started as individual creators in a small town, trying to create photos and videos that have a lasting effect. We all wanted to turn media into our careers, so we came together and started ParvaLuma.

Creative Approach

We approach our work with an open mind and the drive to create something that not only our client likes, but that everyone who sees it can connect with. We focus on the little details that make each company unique and match our work to them so everything fits naturally with the brand.

Studio Philosophy

Shoot what is expected and more. Don't miss the shots your instincts are telling you to get. Work hard and be kind. Be approachable and value others.
